What Inspires Me?

My experience in the MedTech club in highschool

During my four years in high school, I was a member of the MedTech club. This club focused on teaching students about the medical field, helping to immerse me into this field and realize my passion for a career within it.

Designing a prototype for healthcare app

During a Introduction to Computing course at UCSD, I was tasked to design a prototype for a healthcare app. I decided to create a blood pressure tracking app, which not only helped me develop design and computer skills, but showed me how I could utilize these newly developed skills to help people.

My experience volunteering at the San Diego Blood Bank

Being able to witness blood donors use their time to help others truly inspired me. It helped me to recognize my desire to go into the medical field so that my career could revolve around trying to help others lead healthy lives.

Growing up with my mother being a nurse

Growing up with my mother being a nurse was my first introduction to the medical field. As I got older, I began to truly understand the difference she makes in her patients' lives, which ultimately helped me make my decision to pursue a career within the medical field.
